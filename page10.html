CERTIFICATE PROGRAMS

Our certificate programs are crafted to further enhance organizations revolutionize the way they do their businesses in the ever changing environment and to remain on top of their game. Informing our unique approach to program formulation, is our respected faculty’s insights, backed with decades of interdisciplinaryresearch, expertise, and practical real world experience. We further enrich these program development with combination of lessons from our participants’ long years of hands-on corporate, industrial management and leadership experience.  These allow participants’ immediate workplace applications of learning outcomes during and post training sessions.

To open participants to diverse, competitive real world hands on learning experience, these programs incorporate weeks of local and overseas learning visits to factories, organizations and institutions. The overall results are enhanced knowledge/skills for better, and more sustainable organization’s performance.  

These programs are flexibly delivered during one week in a month for 4 months duration, including 1 or more weeks of industry visit locally or overseas, to meet the needs of a particular program

Who should attend
Industry leaders, directors, top managers, and other professionals interested in advancing their capacity and capabilities for better achievements

ADVANCED CERTIFICATE IN MANAGEMENT

Advance Certificate in Management, (ACM), is an intensive and transformative program designed and delivered for top executives managers and industry leaders across all sectors of the economy. It enhances the ability of these businesses and organizations top executives to make significant and desired differences in their establishments. It offers comprehensive interactive experience sharing learning exercise anchored in interdisciplinary team work. Discussions, presentations, role plays, case lets and case studies across corporate key functions of organizations are also offered Complimenting these intents of ACM program, are visits to organizations and interacting with visiting professionals and industry leaders to further cement the embedded hands-on for immediate workplace application. Participants are further exposed to weeks of overseas learning visits for experiential and hands-on training to give opportunity for counterpart interactions and sharing experiences.
This program presents deep review of global analytics of topical issues and industry trends, giving every participant opportunity to understand state of advancement in current markets, contending and emerging theories and practices the world over. It exposes new and valuable techniques informing best practices in decision making processes, human resources management, corporate governance, strategies development and efficient operations options, including financial management. Additional emphasis is placed in critical thinking, innovation and entrepreneurship management to enable participants actively introduce, develop, and actualize new business ideas that advance growth, development and sustainable competitiveness.
