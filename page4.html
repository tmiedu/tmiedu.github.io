Our Driving Principles

These are our driving principles

Asking the questions

Numerous discoveries, inventions and exceptional insights have been attained in the course of pursing better human welfare. While the arguments can continue as to whether all such feats might not have resulted from deliberate research efforts, what is not in contestation today is that, advancing and refining such achievements and creating new ones are largely dependent on responses to research enquiries of why, how, what, when and with what. 

Innovations

Today further improvement in human advancement has intensified and indeed become more challenging, requiring better approaches for better results.
It requires above average thinking, inquiries, including seeking and finding solutions for better economy that will continue to provide the ever increasing diversity in human needs.

Indepth Thinking

At TMI, we explore the liberty to study extraordinary alternatives and deliberately interrogate the value of long appreciated practices. And so, we deploy research-backed disruptive-creative approaches to develop right minds, right brains and right hands to extend the limits of human capabilities to meet current global practical realities of economies.
We remain committed to ensuring balanced technical and management education, upgrading knowledge and skills through research, teaching, experiential training/learning to develop right minds, brains and hands for transformative industrialization of the economy.
