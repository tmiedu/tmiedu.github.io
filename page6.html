Research: Technical and Management

At TMI, we agree that if “Plan A” does not work the alphabet has more 25 letters… we are committed to finding the right one.

LIST OF PROGRAMS REGISTER NOW
Every day, environments in which  organizations operate change and with it come corresponding changes in new demands, new products and intensified quest for better ways of doing things and running organizations. Succeeding in in all these human endeavors, needs deeper enquiries into why, what, when, how and with what to be successful. A well-articulated and pursued research efforts provide viable road maps for organizations to progressively advance the course of better economic growth, development and industrialization. Organizations and businesses may have been recording good achievements all the way, but may lose their winning streak if they hang on to old models to success. What TMI does with you and for you under its research program, is create the right platforms for experts to rigorously examine; what was, what is, when and how all of these have been in such a well-blended  academic and practical interrogations that leads to proffering best ways that will meet current requirements for successes.
