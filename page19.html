Dr. Abukakar M. Bala

Dr Abubakar Muhammed Bala is an accomplished consultant veterinay surgon with over 22 years of extensive practice. He is an animal-production specialist, and agribusiness consultant. Dr Bala works have contributed beneficially to improvement in food quality and security in Nigeria. His research areas are animal production, food processing and agribusiness development. At TMI Dr Bala’s expertise bears on quality of food and agribusness program.
He also holds M.Tech, animal production, (Mina), and many post graduate certificates in business management from Lagos business school.
