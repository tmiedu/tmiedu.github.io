Dr. Clem Ekhato

Dr Clem Ekhator enriches TMI faculty with his expertise. In the past over 2 decades he has a good combination of hands on medical services and academic experience both at the University where he teaches as senior academic in the areas of physiology, medicine and surgry, anathomy and nursing sciences for 10 years. And as clinician and public health consultant. He has for several years worked as member of hospital management team. His research interest is in patience care and the role of technology.
Dr Clem holds MD, MPH M.Sc and his Ph.D is in view. He is amember of several professional bodies including, Nigeria Medical Association, (NMA), Physiological Association of Nigeria, (PSN), Human Physiology and Anathomy Society of Canada, Canadian Society of Therapeutic and Pharmacology, and American Society of Nephology.
