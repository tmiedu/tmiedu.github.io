Mr. CYPRIAN ENWEFAH

Cyprian Enwefah is in TMI with his over 3 decades of teaching, research and management expertise spanning across education banking, business, advertising and oil & gas industries. He held various top management positions in varied functional units, including human resources, finance, clients services, media planning, contract admimistration and environmental management. He founded Janiobooks Limited, a publishing and library services and sits on boards of many companies.
Cyprian fortifies TMI quality with his teaching and research interest in human development, organizational performance, documentation and records management and talent identification. He is a chronicler and authoured many books.
He is a Science teacher, an educationist and holds a post graduate diploma in business administration. Cyprian is also a member of Nigerian Institute of Management, (MNIM)
