Corporate Education

Organizations need resources including  appropriate knowledge and skills to meet challenges of managing successful and sustainable enterprises. TMI comes handy in partnering and working with you. 

We work with corporate organizations, public sector agencies and departments to push the boundaries of performance through providing custom programs that deliver creative learning experiences. We design, prepare, develop and deliver tailor-made programs-solutions that best suits you. 

Our custom programs are flexibly structured and scheduled to run in our venues or in any location agreeable to our client. The duration of these programs span from 1- 7 days, while others run from several weeks to12 months and longer depending on client’s needs; sometimes with embedded projects. These programs are delivered by TMI faculty through experiential and flexible learning schedules, class presentations, interactive discussions, role plays, case lets/case studies and laboratory work, involving teamwork/exercises. We offer you weeks of local and overseas’ factory/organization learning visits for our certificate programs as part of your learning processes.

Open enrollment program
If our custom executive programs do not fit your organization, you are invited to
take interest in sending teams to our executive open enrollment programs.
Attending such course together as a team provides additional benefit of common
platform for understanding and facilitating application of learning outcomes for
greater impact on your organization performance.
